The Tribal frogs

Located near the center of the map. Also known as the Bog lands. The tribal frogs are known to stay true to their old traditional livelihoods and practices. Their bloodlines can be traced back to the oldest races of anurans.

They have a theocratic government; they rely on livestock, hunting and agriculture. They are led by the Shaman King, and they are locally governed by their local chiefs and shamans. The Shaman King's role is to maintain and protect the lives of the all-tribal anurans. During famine, the Shaman King has the power to make their crop produce double.

The Shaman King has the ability to empower and activate its tribal guard when they are in dangerous times. Once a Shaman King dies, it is passed down to a deemed worthy shaman.
